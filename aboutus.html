WHO WE ARE

In 2020, our Table Hoppers team saw how greatly the pandemic had affected a lot of people. Created with the general populace in mind, Table Hoppers is an easy restaurant review website, run by a team of young adults. While we may not have experience in the restaurant industry, our main goal is to share our unbiased experiences and return honest reviews so you don’t have to put yourself at risk. Table Hoppers was created for those who love trying new places but weren’t able to do so because of the risks involved.

We are not affiliated with any restaurants and our monthly recommendations are based off personal choice in together with opinions and recommendations of others both offline and online. We take a lot of factors into consideration when making our recommendations and we hope that you approve of each of our monthly features!

This website is created by people, for people. Feel free to head to our Meet Our Team page if you want to learn more about the individual members, or you can send us a restaurant you’d like us to try or you have questions, you can use our contact page.

Let’s get through this pandemic together, and maybe eventually you can also be your own Table Hopper!
